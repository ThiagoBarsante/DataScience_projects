    •

      2. 

    

    • 

    •

       6.1: filter all Line Item ID with engagement higher than specific threshold (>= xxxx)

       6.2: sort the new data based on lowest CPE to highest CPE

       6.3: apply the rank from lowest CPE to highers CPE
